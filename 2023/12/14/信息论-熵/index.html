熵

熵

概率空间：设\(\mathcal{X}\)是一个样本空间，\(\mathcal{F}\)是\(\mathcal{X}\)上的\(\sigma\)域，P是\(\mathcal{F}\)上的概率测度，则称\((\mathcal{X},\mathcal{F},P)\)为概率空间

熵：设\(X:\mathcal{F}\to [0,1]\)为随机变量，称\(H(X)\)是随机变量X的熵，即为X的不确定度，若X的概率分布为P，\(H(X)\)也可记作\(H(P)\)

  • \(H(X)\)是\(\{P(x)\}\)上的对称连续泛函
  • 设n是一个大于2的正整数，定义 \[ \begin{equation*} h(n):=H({\frac{1}{n},\frac{1}{n},\cdots,\frac{1}{n}}) \end{equation*} \] \(h(n)\)关于n不减

Grouping property：设\(P=\{P_{i,j}:1\le i\le k,1\le j\le n_{i}\},\mathcal{X}=\{x_{i,j}:1\le i\le k,1\le j\le n_{i}\}\)，记第i个组为 \[ \begin{equation*} \{x_{i,1},x_{i,2},\cdots,x_{i,n_{i}}\}\longrightarrow P_{i}=\sum_{j=1}^{n_{i}}P_{i,j} \end{equation*} \] 则有 \[ \begin{align*} H(P)&=H(\{P_{i,j}:1\le i\le k,1\le j\le n_{i}\})\\ &=H(P_{1},P_{2},\cdots,P_{k})+\sum_{i=1}^{k}P_{i}\cdot H(\frac{P_{i,j}}{P_{i}}:1\le j\le n_{i}) \end{align*} \]

定理：设m和n是正整数，则满足 \[ \begin{equation*} h(mn)=h(m)+h(n) \end{equation*} \]

证明：应用Grouping Property 可以得到 \[ \begin{align*} h(mn)&=H(\frac{1}{mn},\frac{1}{mn},\cdots,\frac{1}{mn})\\ &=H(\frac{1}{m},\frac{1}{m},\cdots,\frac{1}{m})+m\frac{1}{m}H(\frac{\frac{1}{mn}}{\frac{1}{m}},\frac{\frac{1}{mn}}{\frac{1}{m}},\cdots,\frac{\frac{1}{mn}}{\frac{1}{m}})\\ &=h(m)+h(n) \end{align*} \]

推论：

  • \(m,n,k\in Z^+,\; h(mnk)=h(m)+h(n)+h(k)\)
  • \(m_{1},m_{2},\cdots,m_{l}\in Z^{+},\;h(\prod_{i=1}^{l}m_{i})=\sum_{i=1}^{l}h(m_{i})\)

定理：设n是正整数，则有 \[ \begin{equation*} h(n)=\log n \end{equation*} \]

证明：设\(m\in Z^+\)，则\(\exists k\in Z^+\)，s.t. \[ \begin{equation*} 2^k\le m<2^k+1 \end{equation*} \] 故可知 \[ \begin{equation*} k=h(2^k)\le h(m)\le h(2^{k+1})=k+1 \end{equation*} \] 令N为任意正整数，则有 \[ \begin{align*} &[N\log n]\le h(n^N)\le [N\log n]+1\\ \Longrightarrow &\frac{1}{N}[N\log n]\le h(n)\le \frac{1}{N}[N\log n]+\frac{1}{N} \end{align*} \] 由于 \[ \begin{align*} &\frac{1}{N}[N\log n]\le \frac{1}{N}N \log n =\log n\\ &\frac{1}{N}[N\log n]+\frac{1}{N}\ge \frac{1}{N}(N\log n -1)+\frac{1}{N}=\log n \end{align*} \] 从而有 \[ \begin{equation*} \left|h(n)-\log n\right|\le \frac{1}{N} \end{equation*} \] 由于N的任意性，知\(h(n)=\log n\)

熵：设\(X\sim P=\{P(x):x\in \mathcal{X}\}\) \[ \begin{equation*} H(X)=\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}P(x)\log \frac{1}{P(x)}=-\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}P(x)\log P(x) \end{equation*} \] 称\(H(X)\)是随机变量X在概率分布P上的熵

自信息：设\(X\sim \{P(x):x\in \mathcal{X}\}\)，称X的自信息为 \[ \begin{equation*} i(X)=-\log P(X) \end{equation*} \]

\(H(X)=E_{P}(i(X))\)

联合熵、条件熵

联合熵：称\(H(X,Y)\)为联合熵，若一组离散随机变量\((X,Y)\sim \{p(x,y):x\in \mathcal{X},y\in \mathcal{Y}\}\) \[ \begin{equation*} H(X,Y) = -\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log p(x,y) \end{equation*} \]

对于多元随机变量，称\(H(X^n)\)为联合熵，若\(X^n = (X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n})\sim \{p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n}):x_{i}\in \mathcal{X},1\le i \le n\}\) \[ \begin{equation*} H(X^n)=-\sum_{x_{i}\in \mathcal{X}}p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})\log p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n}) \end{equation*} \]

联合自信息： 若\((X,Y)\sim \{p(x,y)\}\)，则定义\((X,Y)\)的联合自信息为 \[ \begin{equation*} i(X,Y)=-\log p(X,Y) \end{equation*} \]

类似于单元随机变量，有 - 联合熵\(H(X,Y)\)可以写成 \[ \begin{equation*} H(X,Y)=E_{P_{XY}}i(X,Y) \end{equation*} \] - 联合熵\(H(X^n)\)可以写成 \[ \begin{equation*} H(X^n)=E_{P_{X^n}}i(X^n) \end{equation*} \]

条件熵：若\((X,Y)\sim p(x,y)\)，则条件熵定义为 \[ \begin{align*} H(Y|X)&=\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)H(Y|X=x)\\ &=-\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(y|x)\log p(y|x)\\ &=-\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log p(y|x) \end{align*} \]

  • \(H(Y|X)\ge 0\)
  • \(H(Y|X)\le H(Y)\)

定理：\(H(X,Y)=H(X)+H(Y|X)\)

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} H(X,Y) &= -\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log p(x,y)\\ &=-\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log p(x)p(y|x)\\ &= -\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log p(x)-\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log p(y|x)\\ &= -\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)\log p(x)-\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log p(y|x)\\ &= H(X)+H(Y|X) \end{align*} \]

  • \(H(X)\le H(X,Y)\) 当且仅当 \(H(Y|X)=0\) 等号成立
  • \(H(Y|X)\le H(X,Y)\) 当且仅当 \(H(X)=0\) 等号成立

相对熵、互信息

相对熵：设\(p(x),q(x)\)是概率密度函数，称\(p(x)\)与\(q(x)\)的相对熵(Kullback-Leibler距离)为 \[ \begin{equation*} D(p||q)=\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)\log \frac{p(x)}{q(x)} \end{equation*} \]

  • \(D(p||q)\)也称作散度(divergence)
  • 定义 \(0\log \frac{0}{0}=0\)

Fundamental Inf Inequality：\(D(p||q)\ge 0\)，当且仅当\(p=q\)时等号成立

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} D(p||q)&=\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)\log \frac{p(x)}{q(x)}\\ &= -\log_{2}e \sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)\ln \frac{q(x)}{p(x)}\\ &\ge -\log_{2}e \sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)[\frac{q(x)}{p(x)}-1]\\ &= -\log_{2}e[\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}q(x)-\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x)]\\ &=0 \end{align*} \]

条件相对熵：设\(p(X,Y):=\{p(x,y)\},q(X,Y):=\{q(x,y)\}\)是联合概率分布，则条件相对熵为 \[ \begin{align*} D(p(Y| X)||q(Y|X)|p(X)):=\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}p(x) D(p(Y|X=x)||q(Y|X=x)) \end{align*} \]

互信息：设\(X\)和\(Y\)是两个随机变量，同分布于联合概率\(p(x,y)\)，其边际分布为\(p(x),p(y)\)，则互信息\(I(X;Y)\)是联合概率分布\(p(x,y)\)与边际分布之积\(p(x)p(y)\)的相对熵 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y) &=\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}\sum_{y\in \mathcal{Y}}p(x,y)\log \frac{p(x,y)}{p(x)p(y)}\\ &= D(p(x,y)||p(x)p(y))\\ &= E_{p(x,y)}\log \frac{p(X,Y)}{p(X)p(Y)} \end{align*} \]

我们可以重写互信息\(I(X;Y)\)的定义为 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y) &= \sum_{x,y}p(x,y) \log \frac{p(x,y)}{p(x)p(y)}\\ &= \sum_{x,y}p(x,y)\log \frac{p(x|y)}{p(x)}\\ &= -\sum_{x,y}p(x,y)\log p(x) + \sum_{x,y}p(x,y)\log p(x|y)\\ &= -\sum_{x}p(x)\log p(x) - (-\sum_{x,y}p(x,y)\log p(x|y))\\ &= H(X)- H(X|Y) \end{align*} \] 因此，互信息\(I(X;Y)\)是\(X\)的不确定度在已知\(Y\)的信息下的减少量

由此，我们有 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y) &= H(X) - H(X|Y)\\ &= H(Y) - H(Y|X)\\ &= H(X) + H(Y) - H(X,Y) \end{align*} \] 特别地，我们指出 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;X) = H(X) - H(X|X) = H(X) \end{align*} \]

互信息与熵:

\[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y) &= H(X) - H(X|Y)\\ I(X;Y) &= H(Y) - H(Y|X)\\ I(X;Y) &= H(X) + H(Y) - H(X,Y)\\ I(X;Y) &= I(Y;X)\\ I(X;X) &= H(X) \end{align*} \]

条件互信息：对于随机变量\(Z\)，条件互信息\(I(X;Y|Z)\)定义为 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y|Z) &= \sum_{z\in \mathcal{Z}}p(z)I(X;Y|Z=z) \end{align*} \]

定理：

\[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y|Z) &= H(X|Z) - H(X|Y,Z)\\ &= I(Y;X|Z) = H(Y|Z) - H(Y|X,Z)\\ &= H(X|Z) + H(Y|Z) - H(X,Y|Z) \end{align*} \]

链式法则

熵链式法则：

\[ \begin{align*} H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}) &= \sum_{i=1}^{n} H(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1}) \end{align*} \]

法一： 重复利用二元随机变量的链式法则，有 \[ \begin{align*} H(X_{1},X_{2}) &= H(X_{1}) + H(X_{2}|X_{1})\\ H(X_{1},X_{2},X_{3}) &= H(X_{1}) + H(X_{2},X_{3}|X_{1})\\ &= H(X_{1}) + H(X_{2}|X_{1}) + H(X_{3}|X_{1},X_{2})\\ &\cdots\cdots\\ H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}) &= H(X_{1}) + H(X_{2}|X_{1}) + \cdots + H(X_{n}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n-1})\\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{n} H(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1}) \end{align*} \]

法二：由于 \[ \begin{align*} p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})&= \prod_{i=1}^{n}p(x_{i}|x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{i-1})\\ i(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})&= i(x_{1})+i(x_{2}|x_{1})+\cdots+i(x_{n}|x_{1}x_{2}\cdots x_{n-1}) \end{align*} \] 故 \[ \begin{align*} H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}) &= E_{p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})}i(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n})\\ &= \sum_{x_{i}\in \mathcal{X}_{i}}p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})i(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})\\ &= \sum_{x_{i}\in \mathcal{X}_{i}}p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})i(x_{1})+\sum p(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})i(x_{2}|x_{1})+\cdots\\ &= H(X_{1})+H(X_{2}|X_{1})+\cdots+H(X_{n}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n-1})\\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{n} H(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1}) \end{align*} \]

条件熵链式法则：\(H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}|Y) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} H(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1},Y)\)

互信息链式法则：

\[ \begin{align*} I(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n};Y)=\sum_{i=1}^{n}I(X_{i};Y|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1}) \end{align*} \]

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} I(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n};Y) &= H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}) - H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}|Y)\\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{n} H(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1}) - \sum_{i=1}^{n}H(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1},Y)\\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{n} I(X_{i};Y|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1}) \end{align*} \]

相对熵链式法则：

\[ \begin{align*} &D(p(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n})||q(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}))\\ =&\sum_{i=1}^{n}D(p(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1})||q(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1})|p(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1})) \end{align*} \]

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} &D(p(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n})||q(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}))\\ = & E_{p} \log \frac{p(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n})}{q(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n})}\\ = & E_{p}\Big(\log\frac{p(X_{1})}{q(X_{1})} + \log \frac{p(X_{2}|X_{1})}{q(X_{2}|X_{1})}+\cdots+\log\frac{p(X_{n}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n-1})}{q(X_{n}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n-1})}\Big)\\ = & D(p(X_{1})||q(X_{1})) + D(p(X_{2}|X_{1})||q(X_{2}|X_{1})|p(X_{1}))+\cdots\\ = & \sum_{i=1}^{n}D(p(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1})||q(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1})|p(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1})) \end{align*} \]

琴生不等式及其应用

凸函数：称\(f(x)\)是在\((a,b)\)上是凸函数，若满足\(\forall x_{1},x_{2}\in (a,b),0\le \lambda \le 1\)，有 \[ \begin{align*} f(\lambda x_{1}+(1-\lambda)x_{2}) \le \lambda f(x_{1}) + (1-\lambda)f(x_{2}) \end{align*} \] 称\(f(x)\)是严格凸函数，当且仅当\(\lambda=1\ or\ 0\)时，等号成立

琴生不等式：若\(f(x)\)是凸函数，\(X\)是随机变量，则有 \[ \begin{align*} Ef(X)\ge f(EX) \end{align*} \]

信息不等式：设\(p(x),q(x),x\in \mathcal{X}\)是概率密度函数，则有 \[ \begin{align*} D(p||q) \ge 0 \end{align*} \] 等号成立当且仅当\(p(x)=q(x),\forall x\in \mathcal{X}\)

证明：设\(A=\{x:p(x)>0\}\)，则 \[ \begin{align*} -D(p||q) &= -\sum_{x\in A} p(x)\log\frac{p(x)}{q(x)}\\ &= \sum_{x\in A}p(x)\log\frac{q(x)}{p(x)}\\ &\le \log\sum_{x\in A}p(x)\frac{q(x)}{p(x)}\\ &= \log\sum_{x\in A}q(x) \le \log\sum_{x\in \mathcal{X}}q(x)\\ &= 0 \end{align*} \]

互信息的非负性：对于任意两个随机变量\(X,Y\)，有 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y)\ge 0 \end{align*} \] 等号成立当且仅当\(X,Y\)独立

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y) = D(p(x,y)||p(x)p(y))\ge 0 \end{align*} \] 等号成立当且仅当\(p(x,y)=p(x)p(y)\)，即\(X,Y\)独立

定理：\(D(p(y|x)||q(y|x)) \ge 0\)，等号成立当且仅当 \(p(y|x)=q(y|x)\)

定理：\(I(X;Y|Z) \ge 0\)，等号成立当且仅当\(X,Y\)在\(Z\)下条件独立

最大熵分布：\(H(X)\le \log \left|\mathcal{X}\right|\)，等号成立当且仅当X在\(\mathcal{X}\)上服从均匀分布

证明：设\(u(x)=\frac{1}{\left|\mathcal{X}\right|}\)为\(\mathcal{X}\)上的均匀分布概率函数，\(p(x)\)为\(X\)的概率密度函数，则 \[ \begin{align*} D(p||u)=\sum p(x)\log\frac{p(x)}{u(x)}=\log\left|\mathcal{X}\right|-H(x)\ge 0 \end{align*} \]

定理：\(H(X|Y)\le H(X)\)，等号成立当且仅当\(X,Y\)独立

直观上，这个定理表明知道另一个随机变量\(Y\)能够降低\(X\)的不确定度，同时也只能降低\(X\)的不确定度，值得一提，这仅在平均时成立，特别地，\(H(X|Y=y)\)可能大于或小于或等于\(H(X)\)，但一定有\(H(X|Y)\le H(X)\)

定理：\(H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n})\le \sum_{i=1}^{n}H(X_{i})\)，等号成立当且仅当\(X_{i}\)独立

证明：由熵链式法则 \[ \begin{align*} H(X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{n}) &= \sum_{i=1}^{n}H(X_{i}|X_{1},X_{2},\cdots,X_{i-1})\\ &\le \sum_{i=1}^{n}H(X_{i}) \end{align*} \]

Log Sum 不等式及其应用

对数和不等式：对于非负数\(a_{1},a_{2},\cdots,a_{n},b_{1},b_{2},\cdots,b_{n}\)，有 \[ \begin{align*} \sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{i}\log \frac{a_{i}}{b_{i}}\ge \Big(\sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{i}\Big)\log\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{i}}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}b_{i}} \end{align*} \] 当且仅当\(\frac{a_{i}}{b_{i}}=const\)等号成立

证明：不失去一般性，可设\(a_{i}>0,b_{i}>0\)，设\(f(t)=t\log t\)，可知\(f(t)\)是严格凸函数，由Jensen不等式，有 \[ \begin{align*} \sum\alpha_{i}f(t_{i})\ge f(\sum\alpha_{i}t_{i}),\quad \alpha_{i}\ge 0,\sum_{i=1}\alpha_{i}=1 \end{align*} \] 令\(\alpha_{i}=\frac{b_{i}}{\sum_{j=1}^{n}b_{j}},t_{i}=\frac{a_{i}}{b_{i}}\)

于是得到 \[ \begin{align*} \sum\frac{a_{i}}{\sum b_{j}}\log\frac{a_{i}}{b_{i}}\ge \sum \frac{a_{i}}{\sum b_{j}}\log \sum\frac{a_{i}}{\sum b_{j}} \end{align*} \] 即 \[ \begin{align*} \sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{i}\log \frac{a_{i}}{b_{i}}\ge \Big(\sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{i}\Big)\log\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{i}}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}b_{i}} \end{align*} \]

使用对数和不等式可以证明 \[ \begin{align*} D(p||q) &=\sum p(x)\log \frac{p(x)}{q(X)}\\ &\ge \Big(\sum p(x)\Big)\log \frac{\sum p(x)}{\sum q(x)}\\ &=1\log \frac{1}{1}=0 \end{align*} \]

相对熵的凸性：设\((p_{1},q_{1}),(p_{2},q_{2})\)是两组概率密度函数对，则有 \[ \begin{align*} D(\lambda p_{1}+(1-\lambda)p_{2}||\lambda q_{1}+(1-\lambda)q_{2})\le \lambda D(p_{1}||q_{1})+(1-\lambda)D(p_{2}||q_{2}),\quad \forall \lambda\in[0,1] \end{align*} \]

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} &(\lambda p_{1}(x)+(1-\lambda)p_{2}(x))\log\frac{\lambda p_{1}(x)+(1-\lambda)p_{2}(x)}{\lambda q_{1}(x)+(1-\lambda)q_{2}(x)}\\ \le & \lambda p_{1}(x)\log\frac{\lambda p_{1}(x)}{\lambda q_{1}(x)}+(1-\lambda)p_{2}(x)\log\frac{(1-\lambda)p_{2}(x)}{(1-\lambda)q_{2}(x)}\\ =& \lambda p_{1}(x)\log\frac{p_{1}(x)}{q_{1}(x)}+(1-\lambda)p_{2}(x)\log\frac{p_{2}(x)}{q_{2}(x)} \end{align*} \] 对所有的\(x\)求和即可

熵的凹性：\(H(p)\)是关于\(p\)的凹函数

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} H(p)=\log\left|\mathcal{X}\right|-D(p||u) \end{align*} \] 其中\(u\)是\(\mathcal{X}\)上的均匀概率分布，由\(D(p||u)\)的凸性，可得\(H(p)\)是凹函数

定理：设\((X,Y)\sim p(x,y)=p(x)p(y|x)\)，互信息\(I(X;Y)\)在固定\(p(y|x)\)是关于\(p(x)\)的凹函数，在固定\(p(x)\)是关于\(p(y|x)\)的凸函数

证明：

  • 由于 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y)=H(Y)-H(Y|X)=H(Y)-\sum_{x}p(x)H(Y|X=x) \end{align*} \] 若\(p(y|x)\)固定，则\(p(y)\)是\(p(x)\)的线性函数

    由于\(H(Y)\)是关于\(p(y)\)的凹函数，故\(H(Y)\)也是关于\(p(x)\)的凹函数

    由于\(\sum_{x}p(x)H(Y|X=x)\)是\(p(x)\)的线性函数，不影响凹凸性，从而\(I(X,Y)\)是\(p(x)\)的凹函数

数据处理不等式

马尔科夫链：称随机变量\(X,Y,Z\)构成马尔科夫链\((X\rightarrow Y\rightarrow Z)\)，若满足\(Z\)的条件分布只依赖于\(Y\)，与\(X\)条件独立，即 \[ \begin{align*} p(x,y,z)=p(x)p(y|x)p(z|y) \end{align*} \]

马尔科夫链的条件独立 \[ \begin{align*} p(x,z|y)=\frac{p(x,y,z)}{p(y)}=\frac{p(x,y)p(z|y)}{p(y)}=p(x|y)p(z|y) \end{align*} \]

数据处理不等式：若\(X\rightarrow Y\rightarrow Z\)，则有 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y)\ge I(X;Z) \end{align*} \]

证明：由链式法则，有 \[ \begin{align*} I\left(X;Y,Z\right)& =I\left(X;Z\right)+I\left(X;Y|Z\right) \\ &=I\left(X;Y\right)+I\left(X;Z|Y\right) \end{align*} \] 由于\(X,Z\)条件独立，从而有\(I(X;Z|Y)=0\)，由于\(I(X;Y|Z)\ge 0\)，则 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y)\ge I(X;Z) \end{align*} \]

推论：令\(Z=g(Y)\)，则有 \(I(X;Y)\ge I(X;g(Y))\)

推论：若\(X\rightarrow Y\rightarrow Z\)，则有\(I(X;Y|Z)\le I(X;Y)\)

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} I\left(X;Y,Z\right)& =I\left(X;Z\right)+I\left(X;Y|Z\right) \\ &=I\left(X;Y\right)+I\left(X;Z|Y\right) \end{align*} \] 由于\(I(X;Z|Y)=0,I(X;Z)\ge 0\)，故 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;Y|Z)\le I(X;Y) \end{align*} \]

Fano 不等式

Fano 不等式：对于任意估计\(\hat{X}\)使得\(X\rightarrow Y \rightarrow \hat{X}\)，且\(P_{e}=Pr(X\ne \hat{X})\)，则有 \[ \begin{align*} H(P_e)+P_e\log|\mathcal{X}|\geq H(X|\hat{X})\geq H(X|Y). \end{align*} \]

证明：定义错误随机变量 \[ \begin{align*} \left.E=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}1&\quad\text{if}\;\hat{X}\neq X,\\0&\quad\text{if}\;\hat{X}=X.\end{array}\right.\right. \end{align*} \] 由链式法则 \[ \begin{align*} H(E,X|\hat{X})& =H(X|\hat{X})+\underbrace{H(E|X,\hat{X})}_{=0} \\ &=\underbrace{H(E|\hat{X})}_{\leq H(P_e)}+\underbrace{H(X|E,\hat{X})}_{\leq P_e\log|\mathcal{X}|} \end{align*} \] 由于条件减少熵，故\(H(E|\hat{X})\le H(E)=H(P_e)\)，同时由于\(E\)是\(X,\hat{X}\)的函数，则条件熵\(H(E|X,\hat{X})=0\)，且有 \[ \begin{align*} H(X|E,\hat{X})&=\mathrm{Pr}(E=0)H(X|\hat{X},E=0)+\mathrm{Pr}(E=1)H(X|\hat{X},E=1)\\ &\le (1-P_e)0+P_e \log|\mathcal{X}|\\ &=P_e \log|\mathcal{X}| \end{align*} \] 从而得到 \[ \begin{align*} H(P_e)+P_e\log|\mathcal{X}|\geq H(X|\hat{X}) \end{align*} \] 由于\(X\rightarrow Y\rightarrow \hat{X}\)是马尔科夫链，由数据处理不等式，有 \[ \begin{align*} I(X;\hat{X})\le I(X;Y) \end{align*} \] 因此 \[ \begin{align*} H(X|\hat{X})\ge H(X|Y) \end{align*} \]

推论：对于任意随机变量\(X,Y\)，令\(p=Pr(X\ne Y)\)，则有 \[ \begin{align*} H(p)+p\log |\mathcal{X}|\ge H(X|Y) \end{align*} \]

推论： 令\(P_e = Pr(X\ne \hat{X}),\hat{X}:\mathcal{Y}\rightarrow \mathcal{X}\)，则 \[ \begin{align*} H(P_e) + P_e \log(|\mathcal{X}-1|) \ge H(X|Y) \end{align*} \]

定理：设\(X,X'\)独立同分布，熵为\(H(X)\)，则有 \[ \begin{align*} \Pr(X=X^{\prime})\geq2^{-H(X)}, \end{align*} \] 等号成立当且仅当\(X\)满足均匀分布

证明：设\(X~p(x)\)，由琴生不等式，得 \[ \begin{align*} 2^{E\log p(X)}\leq E2^{\log p(X)}, \end{align*} \] 即 \[ \begin{align*} 2^{-H(X)}=2^{\sum p(x)\log p(x)}\leq\sum p(x)2^{\log p(x)}=\sum p^2(x) = \Pr(X=X^{\prime}) \end{align*} \]

推论： 设\(X,X'\)独立，\(X~p(x),X'~r(x),x,x'\in \mathcal{X}\)，则有 \[ \begin{align*} \Pr(X=X')&\geq2^{-H(p)-D(p||r)},\\\Pr(X=X')&\geq2^{-H(r)-D(r||p)} \end{align*} \]

证明：

\[ \begin{align*} 2^{-H(p)-D(p||r)} &=2^{\sum p(x)\log p(x)+\sum p(x)\log{\frac{r(x)}{p(x)}}} \\ &=2^{\sum p(x)\log r(x)} \\ &\leq\sum p(x)2^{\log r(x)} \\ &=\sum p(x)r(x) \\ &=\Pr(X=X^{\prime}), \end{align*} \]
