数理方程-位势方程

数理方程 位势方程 基本解 调和函数

位势方程

基本解

基本解推导

计算Laplace方程基本解 \[ \bigtriangleup E(r)= \delta(r) \] 注意基本解\(E(r)\)只与\(r=|x|\)有关，因此 \[ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i} = \frac{\partial}{\partial r}\cdot\frac{\partial r}{\partial x_i}=\frac{x_i}{r}\cdot\frac{\partial}{\partial r} \] \[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial^2}{\partial x_{i}^2}=\frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}(\frac{x_i}{r}\cdot\frac{\partial}{\partial r})&=\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}+x_i\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\cdot\frac{x_i}{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial r}(\frac{1}{r})+(\frac{x_i}{r})^2\frac{\partial^2}{\partial r^2}\\ &=(\frac{1}{r}-\frac{x_i^2}{r^3})\frac{\partial}{\partial r}+\frac{x_i^2}{r^2}\frac{\partial^2}{\partial r^2} \end{aligned} \] 从而 \[ \bigtriangleup E = \frac{n-1}{r}\frac{\partial E}{\partial r}+\frac{\partial^2 E}{\partial r^2}=\delta(r) \] 令\(\frac{\partial E}{\partial r}=w(r)\)，可得 \[ w'+\frac{n-1}{r}w=\delta(r) \] 两边乘上\(r^{n-1}\)得到 \[ \frac{d(r^{n-1}w)}{dr}=r^{n-1}\delta(r) \] 由\(\delta\)的广义函数性质，从而有 \[ \frac{d(r^{n-1}w)}{dr}=0\Longrightarrow r^{n-1}w=c \] 即 \[ E'(r)=w=\frac{c}{r^{n-1}} \] 解得 \[ \left\{\begin{matrix} E = c\ln r,\quad n=2 \\ E = c\;r^{2-n},\quad n\ge 3 \end{matrix}\right. \]

基本解

Laplace基本解：对于\(x\in R^{n},x\ne 0\)，Laplace方程的基本解为 \[ \begin{align*} \Gamma(x)=\begin{cases} -\frac{1}{2\pi}\ln \left|x\right|,\quad n=2\\ \frac{1}{n(n-2)|B(0,1)|}\frac{1}{\left|x\right|^{n-2}},\quad n\ge 3 \end{cases} \end{align*} \]

由基本解的性质，对于非齐次位势方程 \[ -\Delta u = f \] 函数 \[ u(x)=\Gamma\ast f = \int_{R^{n}}\Gamma(x-y)f(y)dy \] 是方程的解

调和函数

平均值公式

平均值公式定理：设\(u\in C^2(\Omega)\)为调和函数，则对于任意球\(B(x,r)\subset \Omega\)，满足 \[ u(x)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}u(y)dS_y = \frac{1}{|B(x,r)|}\int_{B(x,r)}u(y)dy \] 证明：

  • 令 \[ \begin{aligned} \phi(r)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}u(y)dS_y \end{aligned} \] 作平移和伸缩变换，则 \[ \begin{aligned} \phi(r)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(0,1)|}\int_{\partial B(0,1)}u(x+rz)dS_z \end{aligned} \] 对\(z\)求微商，由Gauss-Green公式可得 \[ \begin{aligned} \phi'(r) &= \frac{1}{|\partial B(0,1)|}\int_{\partial B(0,1)}\mathcal{D}u(x+rz)zdS(z)\\ &= \frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}\mathcal{D}u(y)\frac{y-x}{r}dS_y\\ &= \frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}\frac{\partial u(y)}{\partial n}dS_y\\ &= \frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{B(x,r)}\bigtriangleup u(y)dy=0 \end{aligned} \] 于是\(\phi(r)\)是一个常数，由\(u(x)\)的连续性，从而 \[ \begin{aligned} u(x)=\lim\limits_{r\to 0^{+}}\phi(r)=\phi(0)=\phi(r) \end{aligned} \] 即 \[ \begin{aligned} u(x)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}u(y)dS_y \end{aligned} \]
  • 注意到 \[ \begin{align*} \int_{B(x,r)}u(y)dy=\int_{0}^{r}\int_{\partial B(x,t)}u(y)dS_ydt \end{align*} \] 再利用上式，则 \[ \begin{align*} \int_{B(x,r)}u(y)dy &= u(x)\int_{0}^{r}|\partial B(x,t)|dt\\ &= |B(x,r)|u(x) \end{align*} \] 即 \[ \begin{align*} u(x)=\frac{1}{|B(x,r)|}\int_{B(x,r)}u(y)dy \end{align*} \]

平均值公式逆定理

平均值公式逆定理：设\(u\in C^{2}(\Omega)\)满足，对任意\(B(x,r)\subset \Omega\)，有 \[ \begin{align*} u(x)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}u(y)dS_y \end{align*} \] 则\(u\)是调和函数

证明：对于固定的\(x\in \Omega\)，任意球\(B(x,r)\subset \Omega\)，令 \[ \begin{align*} \phi(r)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}u(y)dS_y \end{align*} \] 由假设可知，\(\phi(r)\)是一个常数，因此 \[ \begin{align*} \phi'(r)=0 \end{align*} \] 即 \[ \begin{align*} \phi'(r)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{B(x,r)}\bigtriangleup u(y)dy=0 \end{align*} \] 于是 \[ \begin{align*} \frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{B(x,r)}\bigtriangleup u(y)dy=0 \end{align*} \] 令\(r\to 0\)，由\(\bigtriangleup u(x)\)的连续性，则 \[ \begin{align*} \bigtriangleup u(x)=0 \end{align*} \]

极值原理

极值原理：设\(\Omega\)是\(R^{n}\)上的有界开集，\(u\in C^{2}(\Omega)\bigcap C(\overline{\Omega})\)是\(\Omega\)上的调和函数，则 - \(u(x)\)在\(\overline{\Omega}\)上的最大(小)值一定在边界\(\partial\Omega\)上取到，即 \[ \begin{align*} \max_{\overline{\Omega}} u =\max_{\partial \Omega}u \end{align*} \] - 若\(\Omega\)连通，且存在\(x_{0}\in \Omega\)使得调和函数\(u(x)\)在\(x_{0}\)点达到\(u(x)\)在\(\overline{\Omega}\)上的最大(小)值，则\(u\)在\(\overline{\Omega}\)上是常数

证明：仅就最大值的情形证明

  • 令\(w=u+\epsilon e^{x_1}\)，容易验证

    • \(w>u\Longrightarrow \max_{\overline{\Omega}}u\le\max_{\overline{\Omega}}w\)
    • \(\Delta w=\epsilon e^{x_1}>0\)，因而\(w\)的最大值只能在边界上取到，故\(\max_{\overline{\Omega}}w=\max_{\partial\Omega}w\)
    • \(\max_{\partial \Omega}w\le \max_{\partial \Omega}u+\max_{\partial \Omega}\epsilon e^{x_1}\)

    故可得 \[ \begin{align*} \max_{\overline{\Omega}}u\le\max_{\partial \Omega}u+\epsilon\max_{\partial \Omega} e^{x_1} \end{align*} \] 令\(\epsilon\to0\)，则有 \[ \begin{align*} \max_{\overline{\Omega}} u =\max_{\partial \Omega}u \end{align*} \]

  • 假设\(\exists x_{0}\in U\)，满足 \[ \begin{align*} u(x_{0})=M:=\max_{\overline{\Omega}}u \end{align*} \] 因此，存在\(0<r<dist(x_{0},\partial\Omega)\)，运用平均值公式有 \[ \begin{align*} M=u(x_{0})=\frac{1}{|B(x,r)|}\int_{B(x,r)}u(y)dy\le M \end{align*} \] 由此，可以得到对于\(\forall y\in B(x_{0},r)\)，有\(u(y)=M\)，因此，集合\(\{x\in \Omega:u(x)=M\}\)在\(\Omega\)中既开又闭

    由\(\Omega\)是连通的，故\(\{x\in \Omega:u(x)=M\}=\Omega\)，即\(u\)在\(\Omega\)上是常数

刘维尔定理

刘维尔定理：若\(u\)是\(R^{n}\)上的有界调和函数，则\(u\)是常数

证明：设\(\left|u\right|\le M\)，固定\(x\in R^{n}\)，对任意\(r>0\)，在球\(B(x,r)\)上，有 \[ \begin{align*} \left|\mathcal{D}u(x)\right|\le \frac{nC_{1}}{r^{n+1}}\int_{B(x,r)}\left|u(y)\right|dy\le \frac{nC_{1}|B(0,1)|}{r}M \end{align*} \] 令\(r\to \infty\)，有 \[ \begin{align*} \mathcal{D}u(x)\equiv 0,\quad x\in R^{n} \end{align*} \] 因此\(u\)是常数

哈纳克定理

哈纳克定理：设\(\{u_{n}\}\subset C^2(\Omega)\bigcap C(\overline{\Omega})\)是\(\Omega\)上的调和函数列，若\(\{u_{n}\}\)在\(\partial \Omega\)上一致收敛，则\(\{u_{n}\}\)在\(\overline{\Omega}\)上一致收敛，且收敛于一个调和函数

证明：由极值原理知 \[ \begin{align*} \max_{x\in \overline{\Omega}}\left|u_{n}-u_{m}\right|\le \max_{x\in\partial\Omega}\left|u_{n}-u_{m}\right| \end{align*} \] 由于\(\{u_{n}\}\)在\(\partial\Omega\)上一致收敛，可知\(\{u_{n}\}\)在\(\overline{\Omega}\)上一致收敛，记\(\{u_{n}\}\)一致收敛到函数\(u\)

再由于\(u_{n}\)满足平均值公式，即 \[ \begin{align*} u_{n}(x)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}u_{n}(y)dS_y \end{align*} \] 由一致收敛可极限与积分换序，则有 \[ \begin{align*} u(x)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,r)|}\int_{\partial B(x,r)}u(y)dS_y \end{align*} \] 从而\(u(x)\)满足平均值公式，故\(u(x)\)是调和函数

可去奇点定理

可去奇点定理：设\(a\in U\)是调和函数\(u\)的孤立有界奇点，则\(u\)可以延拓为整个\(U\)上的调和函数

证明：只需考察单位化后对\(B=B(0,1)\)，若\(u\)在\(B\setminus\{0\}\)有界调和，\(\partial B\)连续，则能延拓到整个单位球

我们只需证明\(u\)和作为Dirichlet问题的解\(\overline{u}\)在\(B\setminus \{0\}\)上相符即可，其中\(\overline{u}\)满足 \[ \begin{align*} \begin{cases} \Delta \overline{u}=0,\quad \text{in$\;B(0,1)$}\\ \overline{u}=u,\quad \text{on$\;\partial B(0,1)$} \end{cases} \end{align*} \] 先设\(n>2\)，对\(\epsilon>0\)，定义 \[ \begin{align*} v_{\epsilon}(x)=u-\overline{u}+\epsilon(|x|^{2-n}-1) \end{align*} \] 显然有\(\Delta v_{\epsilon}(x)=0\)且\(|x|\to 1\)时，\(v_{\epsilon}(x)\to 0\)，同时\(|x|\to0\)时，\(v_{\epsilon}(x)\to +\infty\)

于是对环状区域\(\{x:\epsilon'<|x|<1-\epsilon'\}\)使用极值原理，令\(\epsilon'\to0^+\)，可知\(v_{\epsilon}\ge 0\)在\(B\setminus\{0\}\)上恒成立

于是令\(\epsilon\to 0^+\)，可知\(u\ge \overline{u}\)，用\(-u\)同理可得\(-u\ge -\overline{u}\)，从而有在\(B\setminus\{0\}\)上 \[ \begin{align*} u=\overline{u} \end{align*} \] 即\(\overline{u}\)为\(u\)在\(B\)上的延拓

格林函数

Dirichlet问题

下面来解决Dirichlet问题 \[ \begin{align*} \begin{cases} -\Delta u=f,\quad in\; \Omega\\ u=g,\quad on\; \partial \Omega \end{cases} \end{align*} \] 为此我们利用基本解来构造格林函数，并以此来获得解的表达式

设\(\Omega\subset R^{n}\)是有界开集且\(\partial\Omega\)光滑，\(u\in C^2(\Omega)\bigcap C^{1}(\overline{\Omega})\)是Dirichlet问题的解

固定\(x\in \Omega\)，取充分小\(\epsilon>0\)，使得\(B(x,\epsilon)\subset \Omega\)，在区域\(V_{\epsilon}:=\Omega\setminus B(x,\epsilon)\)上对\(u(y)\)和基本解\(\Gamma(y-x)\)应用格林公式，则有 \[ \begin{align*} &\int_{V_{\epsilon}}u(y)\Delta \Gamma(y-x)-\Gamma(y-x)\Delta u(y)dy\\ =& \int_{\partial V_{\epsilon}}u(y)\frac{\partial}{\partial \nu}\Gamma(y-x)-\Gamma(y-x)\frac{\partial u}{\partial \nu}(y)dS(y) \end{align*} \] 其中\(\nu\)表示\(\partial V_{\epsilon}\)上的单位外法向量

注意到，当\(x\ne y\)时，\(\Delta\Gamma(x-y)=0\)，且\(\epsilon\to 0\)时，有 \[ \begin{align*} \left|\int_{\partial B(x,\epsilon)}\Gamma(y-x)\frac{\partial u}{\partial \nu}(y)dS(y)\right|\le C\epsilon^{n-1}\max_{\partial B(0,\epsilon)}\left|\Gamma\right|=o(1) \end{align*} \] 进一步还有 \[ \begin{align*} \int_{\partial B(x,\epsilon)}u(y)\frac{\partial}{\partial \nu}\Gamma(y-x)dS(y)=\frac{1}{|\partial B(x,\epsilon)|}\int_{\partial B(x,\epsilon)}u(y)dS(y)\to u(x) \end{align*} \] 从而得到 \[ \begin{align*} u(x)=\int_{\partial \Omega} \Gamma(y-x)\frac{\partial u}{\partial \nu}(y)-u(y)\frac{\partial}{\partial \nu}\Gamma(y-x)dS(y) - \int_{\Omega}\Gamma(y-x)\Delta u(y) dy \end{align*} \] 但是，对于Dirichlet问题来说，\(\frac{\partial u}{\partial\nu}\)仍然未知，下面将通过引进一个调和函数\(\phi^{x}\)来消掉这一项

设对给定\(x\in \Omega\)，函数\(\phi^{x}=\phi^{x}(y)\)满足方程 \[ \begin{align*} \begin{cases} \Delta \phi^{x}(y)=0,\quad y\in \Omega\\ \phi^{x}=\Gamma(y-x),\quad y\in \partial\Omega \end{cases} \end{align*} \] 再次运用格林公式 \[ \begin{align*} -\int_{\Omega}\phi^{x}(y)\Delta u(y)dy &=\int_{\partial\Omega}u(y)\frac{\partial \phi^{x}}{\partial \nu}(y)-\phi^{x}(y)\frac{\partial u}{\partial \nu}(y)dS(y)\\ &= \int_{\partial \Omega}u(y)\frac{\partial \phi^{x}}{\partial\nu}(y)-\Gamma(y-x)\frac{\partial u}{\partial\nu}(y)dS(y) \end{align*} \] 于是有 \[ \begin{align*} u(x)=-\int_{\partial\Omega}u(y)\frac{\partial }{\partial \nu}(\Gamma(y-x)-\phi^{x}(y))dS(y)-\int_{\Omega}(\Gamma(y-x)-\phi^{x}(y))\Delta u(y)dy,\quad (x\in \Omega) \end{align*} \] 记格林函数为 \[ \begin{align*} G(x,y)=\Gamma(y-x)-\phi^{x}(y) \end{align*} \] 从而有 \[ \begin{align*} u(x)=-\int_{\partial\Omega}u(y)\frac{\partial G}{\partial \nu}(x,y)dS(y)-\int_{\Omega}G(x,y)\Delta u(y)dy,\quad (x\in \Omega) \end{align*} \]

对于一般的Dirichlet问题 \[ \begin{align*} \begin{cases} -\Delta u=f,\quad in\; \Omega\\ u=g,\quad on\; \partial \Omega \end{cases} \end{align*} \] 由格林函数构造出的函数\(u\) \[ \begin{align*} u(x)&=-\int_{\partial\Omega}u(y)\frac{\partial G}{\partial \nu}(x,y)dS(y)-\int_{\Omega}G(x,y)\Delta u(y)dy,\quad (x\in \Omega)\\ &=-\int_{\partial\Omega}g(y)\frac{\partial G}{\partial \nu}(x,y)dS(y)+\int_{\Omega}f(y)G(x,y)dy,\quad (x\in \Omega) \end{align*} \]

格林函数性质

对称性：对任意\(x,y\in \Omega,x\ne y\)，有 \[ \begin{align*} G(y,x)=G(x,y) \end{align*} \]

证明：固定\(x,y\in \Omega,x\ne y\)，令 \[ \begin{align*} v(z):=G(x,z),w(z):=G(y,z)\quad(z\in \Omega). \end{align*} \] 则有\(\Delta v(z)=0\; (z\ne x),\Delta w(z)=0\; (z\ne y)\)以及\(w=v=0\; (z\in \partial\Omega)\)

取充分小\(\epsilon>0\)，在区间\(V:=\Omega - [B(x,\epsilon)\bigcup B(y,\epsilon)]\)有 \[ \begin{align*} \int_{\partial B(x,\varepsilon)}\frac{\partial v}{\partial\nu}w-\frac{\partial w}{\partial\nu}vdS(z)=\int_{\partial B(y,\varepsilon)}\frac{\partial w}{\partial\nu}v-\frac{\partial v}{\partial\nu}wdS(z) \end{align*} \] 由\(w\)在\(x\)附近光滑，故 \[ \begin{align*} \left|\int_{\partial B(x,\varepsilon)}\frac{\partial w}{\partial\nu}vdS\right|\leq C\varepsilon^{n-1}\sup_{\partial B(x,\varepsilon)}|v|=o(1)\quad\mathrm{~as~}\varepsilon\to0. \end{align*} \] 另一方面，\(v(z)=\Gamma(z-x)-\phi^{x}(z)\)，\(\phi^{x}(z)\)在\(\Omega\)内光滑，故 \[ \begin{align*} \lim_{\varepsilon\to0}\int_{\partial B(x,\varepsilon)}\frac{\partial v}{\partial\nu}wdS=\lim_{\varepsilon\to0}\int_{\partial B(x,\varepsilon)}\frac{\partial\Gamma}{\partial\nu}(z-x)w(z)dS=w(x), \end{align*} \] 因此，当\(\epsilon\to 0\)时， \[ \begin{align*} &\int_{\partial B(x,\varepsilon)}\frac{\partial v}{\partial\nu}w-\frac{\partial w}{\partial\nu}vdS(z)\to w(x)\\ &\int_{\partial B(y,\varepsilon)}\frac{\partial w}{\partial\nu}v-\frac{\partial v}{\partial\nu}wdS(z)\to v(y) \end{align*} \] 于是\(w(x)=v(y)\)，即 \[ \begin{align*} G(x,y)=G(y,x) \end{align*} \]
